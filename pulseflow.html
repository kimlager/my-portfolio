Pulseflow

Pulseflow is a smart, AI-powered digital tool that uses pulse data to promote healthier lifestyles and support UN Goal 3: Good Health and Well-being. It’s all about helping people manage stress, boost fitness, and improve their overall health with real-time tracking and personalized insights. Designed with users in mind, Pulseflow focuses on creating an easy, engaging experience through tailored features and a functional prototype. By combining innovation and AI, it’s a step towards making better health accessible for everyone.
